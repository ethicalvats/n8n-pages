On-Page Technical Audit - https://de-saas.com

  • Critical Issues – Must be fixed immediately:

  • Missing Alt Text on Images: The <img> tag for the logo does not have an alt attribute, which is important for accessibility and image indexing by search engines.

  • Title Tag Length: The title tag "De-SAAS" lacks keywords that describe the content effectively; consider using more descriptive keywords.

  • Meta Description Length: The meta description provided is too short and could be more compelling to improve click-through rates. Aim for 150-160 characters.

  • Quick Wins – Easy fixes with a big impact:

  • Use of Heading Tags: Ensure that heading tags (H2, H3) follow a hierarchical structure. The current layout has lower-level headings (H3) that do not follow H2s appropriately.

  • Optimize Button Text: The "Get Early Access" button is repeated multiple times. This could cause confusion; consider varying the CTAs slightly on different sections.

  • Mobile Optimization: Review the mobile responsiveness of existing design elements like buttons and text size to ensure they are touch-friendly and legible.

  • Opportunities for Improvement – Require more effort but offer potential benefits:

  • Schema Markup Implementation: Implement appropriate schema markup (e.g., FAQ schema, Organization schema) to improve visibility in search results.

  • Content Optimization: There could be a more detailed content strategy to enrich the page with relevant keywords and topics related to micro-SaaS that could drive organic traffic.

  • Loading Speed Optimization: Review scripts and stylesheets to combine or minify where possible, and defer or asynchronously load non-critical resources to enhance page load speed.

  • Internal Linking Strategy: Create a strong internal linking structure by linking to relevant pages or blog posts within the site to improve navigation and SEO.

Recommendations
